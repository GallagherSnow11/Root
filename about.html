About Me

Mosiah 2:41

I was born in Salt Lake City to Garth and Ane Snow. When I was 3 years old, we moved our east to the nothern suburbs of Chicago, where I spent the majority of my life.

I loved living in Chicago, but halfway through senior year, my dad told me and my three younger siblings, it was time to go back west. We moved to Sandy, Utah in Salt Lake County, close to lots of family.

I was not very happy with the move. Yes, we were closer to family, but I had to start over at a brand new school.

Luckily I did not have to stay for too long, becasue a few short months after we moved, I was called to serve a full time mission to Stockholm Sweden. I learned Swedish and taught people about Jesus Christ for 25 months and I could not ask for a better experience.

When I came home, I jumped into school and just over a year after returning I married the love of my life. As of Decemebr 2022, Emma and I have been married for 2 months and could not be happier.
